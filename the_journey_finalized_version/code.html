The Journey

Merging the Human Element and Cultivation.

From Hanoi to New York City

My narrative began in the vibrant, bustling streets of Hanoi, where the aroma of street food and the hum of motorbikes taught me the beauty of organized chaos. Growing up in Vietnam instilled in me a deep appreciation for heritage, community, and the persistent drive to grow despite any obstacle.

Transitioning to the electric energy of New York City wasn't just a change of scenery; it was a profound shift in perspective. Here, among the soaring skyscrapers and the intersection of global cultures, I found a new canvas for my ambitions. This cross-continental journey has shaped my unique lens—combining Eastern values of harmony and patience with Western innovation and grit.
