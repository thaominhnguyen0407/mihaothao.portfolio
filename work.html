01 Consumer Research Solo Project

P&G: Head & Shoulders — Gen Z Has a Gender Problem

Primary mixed-methods research exposing how beauty standards and informal trial behavior drive Gen Z's quiet abandonment of H&S.

psychology The Insight

"H&S becomes Gen Z's first anti-dandruff shampoo because mothers choose it — not the user. Gen Z isn't brand-loyal — they're bottle-locked."

The Challenge

H&S leads global anti-dandruff but faces a threat: Gen Z females abandon the brand perceived as masculine and clinical. It's a parent's product, not a personal choice.

The Strategy

Reposition from "anti-dandruff shampoo" to confidence-restoring scalp care. Soft & Pure (Female Beauty Seeker) and Active Clean (Male Quick Fixer).
